Conditioned Sampling Mode (CSM) — Preregistered Protocol

CSM tests whether the preregistered motif bundle (M4 ∧ M5 ∧ M6 + high M1) defines a high-realization-density region of admissible DAG space.

🔒 Protocol Lock

Fixed Parameters: S=5000 seeds, Q₀.₈₀ percentile, bundle = [M4∧M5∧M6 + high M1]

No-Escape Clause: Cannot change bundle, percentile, seed count, or evaluation parameters after analysis begins.

Success Criteria: E1 (θ̂_B2 ≥ 0.01) AND E2 (θ̂_B2 / θ̂ ≥ 10×) | Decisive: θ̂_B2 ≥ 0.05

⚠ Doctrinal Compliance

CSM performs structural conditioning, NOT optimization. Sampling is uniform within bins. No adaptive search, no utility-directed iteration, no early stopping based on outcomes.
