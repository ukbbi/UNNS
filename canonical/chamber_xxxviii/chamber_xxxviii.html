Chamber XXXVIII: Substrate Internalization (Phase H)

Purpose: Test whether τ-field convergence regimes persist when substrate resolution is increased through bounded internal structure.

Real Observables (Dynamic)

  • Admissibility (A): 1 / (1 + mean(|∇τ|²)) — field smoothness measure
  • Omega-drift (Dω): |Δmean(τ)| + |Δvar(τ)| — temporal change in statistics
  • Stability (S): exp(-mean((τₙ - τₙ₋₁)²)) — inverse field change magnitude

Hypotheses

  • H1 (Structural Survival): τ-field convergence regimes persist under internal structure expansion
  • H2 (μ Classification): μ deviation is either reducible with depth or structurally bounded

Interpretation

H1 (RPI):

  • PRESERVED — Regime signature within threshold
  • SHIFTED — Regime changed but remains admissible
  • COLLAPSED — Admissibility or stability failure (relative to baseline)

H2 (μ Trend):

  • REDUCIBLE — φ-error decreases with depth
  • BOUNDED — φ-error plateaus (structural limit)
  • UNDEFINED — H1 collapsed or trend unstable
