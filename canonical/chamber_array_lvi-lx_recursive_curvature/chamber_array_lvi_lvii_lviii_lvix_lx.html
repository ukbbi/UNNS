LVI was designed as a binary fork: does recursion compound (Theorem 2) or saturate (Theorem 1)? 2. The LVII intervention and its limits

LVII introduced α damping with a critical methodological constraint: σ_F is locked at α=1 and reused across all α. This prevents the calibration from adapting to damping and ensures that passing the nondegeneracy gate at lower α is a genuine signal, not a calibration artifact. α*(F) (the critical damping threshold) is a falsifiable prediction: any family for which no α achieves SATURATING_OR_BOUNDED has compounding intrinsic to its curvature geometry, not its bias.
